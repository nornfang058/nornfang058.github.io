The IAI KFIR

The IAI Kfir is an Israeli modification of the French Dassault Mirage 5 jet fighter, with a new General Electric J-79 engine (the same engine on the F-4 Phantom), and add-on canards bolted to the fuselage to enhance maneuverability. 

Multiple variants of the Kfir were produced and exported, with the US Air Force receiving Kfirs for training and evaluation purposes.

Background: A Kfir Canard diving from the clouds with the airbrake on the wings extended.

The F-16D 
Barak II

The F-16D Barak II "Lightning" is an Israeli modification of the venerable F-16 fighter jet, with new indigenous weaponry and improved avionics.

The F-16D is a twin-seater model of the second-generation F-16C.

Background: A Barak II going supersonic near ground level in the mountains, equipped with the DERBY ARH missile.

The MERKAVA 4M

The Merkava is an Israeli MBT, designed in the Cold War and designed with crew protection as the most important aspect of its design.

The Merkava 4M is one of the most modern iterations of the Merkava MBT, being equipped with the advanced Trophy hardkill APS system.

Background: A Merkava 4M traversing across the desert plains near a city.
